What is burnout?

Swimming is a serious commitment that requires hours of effort and takes a toll on the mental health of athletes. Being a student athlete, takes up time that could be dedicated to school.

Main causes

So, what causes burnout? Often lack of support, focusing too hard and unattainable goals. As well as other anxiety driven factors like, parental pressure to perform, feelings of obligation and being scared of letting down your team,or over-training and lack of recovery. All in which are the perfect recipe for burnout.

"HELP! How do i fix it?"

If you ever find yourself struggling with burnout, implementing a few strategies such as these may help you to regain your motivation:

  • Rest more
  • Set small attainable goals
  • Balance responsibilities and commitments
  • Keep training interesting and introduce variety into your routine
  • Communicate with your coach
