Dryland Training

Swimmers also have to train on land to build strength. This can be helpful when a pool is not available.

Suggested excersises:

  • Burpees: Increase arm, hamstring strength and propulsion.
  • Jump Lunges: Stregthen lower body and improve balance.
  • Donkey Kicks: Activates shoulder and core muscles and doesn't put strain on joints
  • Leg Lifts: Stregthen the lower abdominal muscles and hip flexors.
  • Pushups: Stregthen chest and shoulders, also good for scapular and core.
  • Pullups: Helps improve pull when swimming by strengthening lats.
  • Weighted & Jump Squats: Mimic resistence of water on land and propulsion.
  • Jump Rope: Develops fast twitch fibers with low impact as well as stregthening stabiliser muscles in the wrist, forearm and elbow.
  • Medicine Ball: Stregthens core and increases rage of motion and felxibility in joints.
  • Flutter Kicks: Works lower abs, glute, hip flexors and quads.
