Technique

Swimming with the correct technique is crucial to not only performance, but also enjoyment in the sport. Without the correct technique, injuries are more likely. Reach faster times with better technique. Having knowledge on what will help you to pull the most water and putting it into practice will assist in dropping time, putting you in a better position during a race.

My ranking of strokes and reasoning

  1. Freestyle

    2. Pros:

    • Fastest & most efficient
    • Good for long distance

    Cons:

    • Pull
  2. Backstroke

    3. Pros:

    • Can BREATHE
    • Good for recovery and warm down
    • Low effort

    Cons:

    • Risk of concussion :)
    • Can't swim straight outdoors
  3. Breaststroke

    4. Pros:

    • Good line of sight
    • Easiest stroke to learn

    Cons:

    • Knee joints will fail you
  4. Butterfly

    5. Pros:

    • None.
    • Go fast... but at what cost?

    Cons:

    • Death.
