health Benefits

Swimming provides many health benefits. Including, building of endurance, cardiovascular fitness, muscle strength and flexibility. The universal sport is also easy on the joints, making it a dynamic sport for all ages; especially for older individuals.

Training sessions improve mood. The body releases a chemical called endorphins. This chemical helps the body deal with stress and pain even hours after exiting the pool.

Increases blood flow to the brain improving:

  • Memory
  • Mood
  • Concentration
